Bio

I’m an Applied Physics and R&D Engineer with a background in aircraft systems, experimental optics, hydraulics, and electronics.

After 8 years working on F/A-18 aircraft in the U.S. Navy and defense industry, I earned a technical degree in Electrical Engineering, an AS in Aeronautical Science, and a BS in Physics. During my time at the University of Texas I worked in Dr. Xuan Zhou’s optics lab on spectroscopy of nanomaterials under high pressure, which resulted in several publications and custom engineered solutions. I now operate Mikolaichik Solutions LLC, a small engineering and scientific company focused on product development, advanced composite materials, and high field electronics.

I enjoy conceptualizing solutions to problems on the drawing board, validating them through calculation, and then engineering, sourcing, and assembling the physical hardware. I like the challenge, creativity, and spirit of collaboration involved in determining the best path forward.
