Bio

I’m a Texas-based Applied Physics & Test Engineer with a background in aircraft systems, experimental optics, hydraulics, and electronics.

After 8 years working on F/A-18 aircraft in the U.S. Navy and defense industry, I earned a B.S. in Physics from UTSA, where I worked in Dr. Xuan Zhou’s optics lab on spectroscopy of nanomaterials under high pressure. I now run Mikolaichik Solutions LLC, a small engineering and scientific solutions company focused on practical hardware and experimental systems.

I enjoy designing, building, testing, and iterating on physical systems – whether that’s an optical setup, a robotics platform, or a piece of hardware that solves a real-world problem.
